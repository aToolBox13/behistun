Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2026

What Behistun does

Behistun is a Chrome extension that translates web pages using the Google Translate API. Text from the active tab is sent to a secure proxy server, which forwards it to Google Translate, and returns the translation to your browser.

What data we collect

We collect no personal data. Behistun does not:

  • Store any text you translate
  • Track which pages you visit
  • Collect your IP address or location
  • Require an account or login
  • Use cookies or local analytics

How translation works

Text from the page you are translating is sent to a Cloudflare Worker proxy, which calls the Google Translate API. The proxy does not log or store any translated content. Google's own privacy policy applies to text processed by their API: policies.google.com/privacy.

Permissions

Behistun requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — to read and modify the current tab's content for translation
  • storage — to save your language and feature preferences locally
  • scripting — to inject the translation script into the page
  • contextMenus — to add a right-click "Translate with Behistun" option

Third-party services

Behistun uses Google Translate API for translation. Text you translate is subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue on the extension's GitHub repository.
